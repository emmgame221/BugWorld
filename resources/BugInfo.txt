Ant:
The biggest threat to picnics and farms the world has ever seen.
This bug moves and eats at a steady pace, but the real danger is 
how it guides other bugs to the food.

Ladybug:
It may look cute, but this bug is fast and dangerous.
While not the best at finding food, it eats and moves 
faster than the other bugs.

Stinkbug:
Be more careful about the horrible smell that this bug 
brings with it.
Instead of eating this bug releases a cloud of stink to 
summon nearby ladybugs to the food.

Prestige:
Once players have reached Level 10 they can now prestige. 
Resetting the game and earning gold for every 10 Levels they have cleared
